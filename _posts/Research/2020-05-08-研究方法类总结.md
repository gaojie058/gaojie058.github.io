# 如何做访谈分析？
### 问题
- 需要一套主流的分析方法来对结果进行分析，从而筛选出相对客观或者说无偏见的理论
- 目前了解到的分析理论有**扎根理论、主题分析**
- 文献里获得的分析软件有**MaxQDA、nVivo**(QDA is Qualitative Data Analysis)

[扎根理论](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grounded_theory): Grounded Theory(GT)
uses inductive reasoning(归纳推理) in contrast to the hypothetico-deductive model(假设演绎模型) of the scientific method. 
 As more data is collected, and re-reviewed, codes can be grouped into concepts, an then into categories.
These categories may become the basis for new theory.

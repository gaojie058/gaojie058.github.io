## Methodology for Empirical Verification of the Framework
### Qualitative Approach
- **background:** impact, focus, practice, involvement, role and policy
- **software and hardware requirements:** use, system, programs, services, network, advantages, internet, application and user
- **effects of its application:** distance, structure, efficiency, range, device, mechanism and operation
- **emerging codes:** where additional aspects that were reported by the interviewees were captured
### Quantitative Approach
- [Latent variables](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latent_variable)
- RMSEA: Root Mean Square Error of Approximation
### among the variables that are related to lifestyle, which affects telework. Favourable incidence of the first three variables and the unfavorable incidence of the last two
- Career development
- Discussing or investigating cars
- playing sports or exercising
- carrying out the volunteer or charity work
- watch television, movies or series
### among the variables that are related to the cultural traits that affect telework. The first five is more important.
- ‘If a partner receives an award, I would be proud’
- ‘The well-being of my classmates is important to me
- ‘For me, it is pleasant to spend time with others’
- ‘I feel good when I cooperate with others’
- ‘It is important for me to respect the decisions made by my group’
- ‘I prefer to depend on myself than on others’
- ‘My personal identity is very important to me, and independent of others’
- ‘When someone else does better than me, I get tense’;
- ‘It is my duty to take care of my family, even when I have to sacrifice what I want’
- ‘Family members must stay together, no matter what sacrifices are required’

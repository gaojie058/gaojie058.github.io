# Definition of telecommuting
- narrow definition: telecommuting or “telework” (Watad and DiSanzo, 2000; Nilles,
1992; Olson, 1983) encompasses those opportunities that workers are given to work from home rather than reporting to a centralized office location
- occasional home workers
- mobile workers

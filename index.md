I will be a postdoctoral researcher at [Singapore-MIT Alliance for Research and Technology](https://smart.mit.edu/) starting from early 2024. I will be working with Prof. [Thomas W. Malone](https://mitsloan.mit.edu/faculty/directory/thomas-w-malone). My primary focus will be on exploring the interaction modes in human-Large Language Model (LLM) collaboration. I will also continue my research on creating AI applications and fostering trustworthy AI, particularly in qualitative analysis.

Specifically, I am passionate about: 

- <span style="color:#61C2A2">**AI4SocialScience**</span>: exploring efficient, automated methods and tools to enhance qualitative analysis, with a special focus on collaboration; 
- <span style="color:#EDAEC0">**Trustworthy AI**</span>: developing reliable AI-assisted applications for qualitative coding, and examining trust dynamics between humans and AI within the scope of qualitative analysis.
- <span style="color:#9F92D0">**LLMs+Interaction**</span>: creating applications powered by LLMs, and investigating the various modes of interaction between humans and LLMs.
